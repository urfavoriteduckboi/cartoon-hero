V from V for Vendetta

About V

A political activist vigilante known for his symbol of V as well as his Guy Fawkes mask, known for starting a revolution among the masses of England in order to overthrow a tyrannical government

V's characteristics

  1. Image of hope
  2. Strikes fear into his foes
  3. Mysterious and unruly, most likely a chaotic good type of character
